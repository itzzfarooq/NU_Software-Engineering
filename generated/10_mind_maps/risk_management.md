```
╔══════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════╗
║                           RISK MANAGEMENT                               ║
╠══════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════╣
║  Identifying, analyzing, planning, and monitoring project risks         ║
╚══════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════╝

  │
  ├── Definition ──────────────────────────────────────────────────────────
  │   ├── Risk: Uncertain event with probability & impact on objectives
  │   ├── Risk vs Issue: Risk = potential future problem, Issue = current
  │   └── Risk vs Uncertainty: Risk has known probability, Uncertainty unknown
  │
  ├── Risk Categories ────────────────────────────────────────────────────
  │   ├── Project: Schedule slippage, Budget overrun, Scope creep
  │   ├── Technical: Technology immaturity, Integration, Performance
  │   ├── Product: User rejection, Feature deficiencies, Reliability
  │   └── Business: Strategic changes, Competition, Regulatory, Vendor
  │
  ├── Risk Management Paradigm ───────────────────────────────────────────
  │   ├── Systematic: Continuous, proactive, integrated with PM
  │   ├── Cycle: Identification -> Analysis -> Planning -> Monitoring -> Control
  │   ├── Attitude: Averse, Neutral, Seeking
  │   └── Tolerance: Acceptable level, Threshold for action
  │
  ├── Risk Exposure ──────────────────────────────────────────────────────
  │   ├── Probability: Likelihood (Very Low 0.1 to Very High 0.9)
  │   ├── Impact: Consequence (Very Low 0.05 to Very High 0.8)
  │   ├── Calculation: Exposure = Probability x Impact
  │   └── Assessment: Risk matrix, Ranking, Prioritization
  │
  ├── Risk Strategies ────────────────────────────────────────────────────
  │   ├── Avoidance: Eliminate risk, change plan, do not proceed
  │   ├── Mitigation: Reduce probability or impact, implement controls
  │   ├── Transfer: Insurance, Outsourcing, Fixed-price contracts
  │   ├── Acceptance: Acknowledge, contingency plan, management reserve
  │   └── Exploitation: Ensure positive risk occurs, maximize benefits
  │
  ├── Risk Identification Techniques ─────────────────────────────────────
  │   ├── Brainstorming: Team sessions, creative thinking
  │   ├── Checklists: Historical data, organizational templates
  │   ├── Interviews: Expert consultation, stakeholder input
  │   ├── SWOT: Strengths, Weaknesses, Opportunities, Threats
  │   ├── Delphi: Anonymous experts, iterative feedback
  │   └── Root Cause Analysis: Fishbone diagrams, underlying causes
  │
  └── Risk Monitoring ────────────────────────────────────────────────────
      ├── Risk Register: Document all risks, track status, record actions
      ├── Risk Audits: Review process, assess effectiveness
      ├── Reassessment: Periodic review, update P&I, identify new risks
      └── Metrics: Trends, exposure changes, mitigation effectiveness

```
